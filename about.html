CAFE • BRASSERIE • BAR

Step into Café Zoe in the day, and you will be delighted with the radiant sunlight streaming through big glass windows, exposed brick walls and high ceilings over a 4000 sq. ft. space. This Café, Brasserie and Bar has retained its industrial heritage and offers free wi-fi, live jazz concerts, photography exhibitions and more. The menu delights with authentic European cuisine, an all day breakfast and cocktails. Some of the menu specialties are akin to a fine dining restaurant but at value for money prices. It is also the perfect place to enjoy a nice Sunday brunch with food critics claiming we have ‘kick ass burgers’! Energetic and buzzing in the evenings Café Zoe is the perfect evening hang out. Transforming into a perfect party destination over the weekends, Café Zoe is the obvious choice and is soon becoming one of Bombay’s legendary restaurants. This delightful space has been named one of the world's hottest bars in 24 cities across the world by CONDE NAST TRAVELERS and has also won the prestigious TIMES NOW jury award for the Best Debut restaurant in India and TIMES FOOD & NIGHTLIFE award for best European/Global restaurant in 2013. It has also been featured in the MIELE GUIDE 2013 as one of Asia's top 500 restaurants. In 2014, it was voted ZOMATO users choice awards for the Best All Day Diner and has been awarded with Certificates of Excellence by TripAdvisor for consistently earning top ratings from TripAdvisor travellers in 2014 and 2015. In 2016 Café Zoe was awarded THE TIMES FOOD AWARD under the category ‘Casual Dine (South Mumbai) – Best European/Global’. And THE INDIA NIGHTLIFE CONVENTION AND AWARDS for Best Resto Bar and Best Café Bar in Mumbai and Best Resto Bar in India. This year Cafe Zoe has won THE TIMES FOOD AWARDS 2017 in the category of Best European in Casual Dining and has been voted in the Top 6 European Restaurants in Mumbai by BURRP. The most recent Award was the INCA Award for the Best Café Bar in Mumbai and Nationally.
